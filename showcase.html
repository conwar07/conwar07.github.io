The Elucidator

The Elucidator is a black sword dropped by a demonic class monster on the 50th floor. It was primarily wielded by Kirito, the black swordsman.
The blade was later improved to a +45 ranking by the blacksmith lizbeth alongside the forging of kiritos other primary weapon the dark repulsor.
Class= One handed long sword

The Dark Repulser

The dark repulser is a light blue coloured sword made from Crystallite ingots. Forged by Lizbeth the blacksmith and wielded by Kirito
alongside the Elucidator. The weapon was destroyed during Kiritos fight with Akihiko Kayaba on the 75th floor.
Class= One handed long sword

Lambent Light

Lambent Light is a one handed rapier wielded by lightning flash Asuna of the guild knights of the blood oath.
The rapier was forged and improved along the course of the game by the blacksmith Lizbeth. Lambent Light was wielded
by Asuna during the final battle on the 75th floor.
